My Miscellany

My name's Dave, and this Miscellany is supposed to be a venture into being able to share a multitude of information I've gathered. I collect autographed trading cards and similar memorabilia and wanted a place to be able to show off my collection. I've also been a historical reenactor, a soccer referee, a woodworker, a paralegal, and a firearm instructor. I've worked at an oil refinery and have published a book. I assumed that it would be worthwhile to include links on this miscellany about other things that I have found interesting over the course of my life. It also felt like a good use of static webpages - rather than attempting to use Wordpress or similiar. I hope that something in these pages brings you joy!
